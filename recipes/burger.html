Burger

There is nothing like taking a bite into a juicy burger. Here is a recipe that is a spin on the classic dish.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 lbs of ground beef
  • Chopped onion
  • Montreal steak seasoning

Steps

  1. Mix ground beef with chopped onion and Montreal steak seasoning in a large bowl
  2. Form burger patties after working the mixture thoroughly
  3. Put burger patties on George Foreman grill for 4–5 minutes or until cooked as desired
