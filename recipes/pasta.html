Pasta

Pasta is a staple meal that is filling and hearty. Here is a recipe that makes it creamy and adds some ground beef to the mix.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1/2 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (14.5 oz) diced tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
  • 1 tablespoon Italian seasoning
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 pound pasta

Steps

  1. Boil a salted pot of water for the pasta and cook it al dente according to package directions.
  2. In a skillet, sautC) the onion in olive oil until softened.
  3. Add in the ground beef and garlic and continue cooking until browned.
  4. Spoon out any excess fat if needed.
  5. Stir in the diced tomatoes, tomato paste, Dijon mustard, and Italian seasoning.
  6. Simmer until the sauce has reduced somewhat.
  7. Stir in the heavy cream and let it warm through for a few minutes.
  8. Generously season the sauce with salt and pepper, then toss with the drained pasta.
  9. Top with fresh parmesan if desired.
