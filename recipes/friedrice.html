Fried Rice

Here is a recipe for Fried Rice that you can make quickly.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • 1/2 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 120 grams bacon, chopped (optional, or use ham)
  • 1 1/2 cups vegetables, diced (frozen or fresh) B3
  • 2 cups day-old white rice, cooked, refrigerated or frozen defrosted, packed B3
  • 3 whisked eggs
  • 3/4 cup green onions (shallots/scallions), sliced B3
  • 1 tablespoon Chinese cooking wine (sub Mirin, cooking sake or dry sherry) B3
  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce
  • 1 tablespoon light soy sauce (or all-purpose) B3
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil (optional)
  • 1/4 teaspoon white pepper

Steps

  1. Boil a salted pot of water for the rice and cook it according to package directions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a wok or skillet, heat the oil over high heat.
  3. Add the onion and garlic and stir-fry for 1 1/2 minutes.
  4. Add the bacon and continue stir-frying until browned.
  5. Add the vegetables and stir-fry for another 1–2 minutes.
  6. Add the rice and stir-fry for 2–3 minutes.
  7. Move everything to the side and scramble the eggs in the wok or pan.
  8. Add the green onions, Chinese cooking wine, oyster sauce, soy sauce, sesame oil, and white pepper.
  9. Stir-fry for another 2–3 minutes until everything is heated through.
  10. Serve immediately.
